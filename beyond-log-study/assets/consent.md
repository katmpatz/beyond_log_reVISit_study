Aviz Team – Inria Saclay  

**Project name:**  
Comparison of visualizations for large-value ranges  

You are invited to take part in this project, whose purpose is to evaluate which visualization design is more suitable for values that span multiple orders of magnitude.  
The Aviz team conducts research in the field of visualization, and the results of these experiments will help us improve visualization design and research in this field more generally.  
This study will take place online.

#### **PRIVACY AND DATA PROTECTION ACT**

Inria is very attentive and vigilant regarding personal data protection (DCP) issues.  
It has appointed a Data Protection Officer (DPO) responsible for ensuring Inria’s compliance with the European General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

**Personal data is collected and processed for the following purposes:**  
We will collect three types of data:

1. **Experiment data:** completion time, accuracy, subjective confidence, and qualitative feedback for each trial.  
2. **Identification and background data:** prolific ID, gender identification, age, and familiarity with logarithmic scales.

The study data will be logged in real time and stored on an encrypted server.  
Each day, log files will be downloaded to encrypted Inria computers.  
The dataset will include Prolific participant identifiers, which will be removed before analysis to ensure full anonymization.

In accordance with Article 13 of the Regulation (EU) 2016/679 (GDPR), please find below information about this data collection:
